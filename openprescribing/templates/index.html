Explore GP prescribing data

Every month the NHS publishes anonymised open data about the drugs prescribed by GPs in England. But the raw data files are large and unwieldy. We're making it easier for CCGs, GPs and everyone to use - helping prescribers spot trends, savings and safety improvements.

For CCGs

Compare yourself with other CCGs

Use our flexible query form to compare a CCG visually with other CCGs. Or see how all your practices compare to other CCGs.

Quick overview

Get a quick overview of how you're doing on six major prescribing indicators, compared to the national mean.

For GPs

Compare your practice with others

Use our flexible query form to compare a practice visually within a CCG. Or compare yourself with practices elsewhere.

Quick overview

Get a quick overview of how you're doing on six major prescribing indicators, compared to the national mean.

For everyone

Spot national trends

See overall spending on particular drugs - like the fall in Tamiflu prescribing since 2011, or trends in anti-depressant prescribing. Coming soon! Get a quick visual overview of all practices in the country.

Get the data

Are you an analyst or a coder? Use our data API to download CSVs. Query anything you like, and get the data with a few clicks.
